À PROPOS DE NOUS :

Some informations

It's a Mexican food truck based in London near the Mexican embassy where we can serve you some "Xtremely" spicy Mexican dishes for pepper lovers !

Our story

My brother and i were born in London but our parents are from Mexico. We were raised in the Mexican culture, that's why we love a lot this type of food and we want to share it with anyone who wants. So, we decided to create our own restaurant, but as you can imagine, London is full of restaurants...but one day we heard about "Food Trucks". A way to realise our dreams even if we didn't have a fix place to build our restaurant. So, during the past few months, we have created the perfect Mexican food truck where you can eat our Mexican dishes made from our mother's famous recipes

Our commitments

We try our best to recycle the most we can, our bags and plates are made of recycled cardboard, we source from local producers and we have a minimalist menu to avoid waste.
